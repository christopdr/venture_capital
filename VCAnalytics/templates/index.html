VC-Startup Intro

For 2050 the global population will reach 9.8 billion according to the UN, there will be a strong pressure for new products and demand for water, energy and food while fertility rates are decreasing in developed countries, baby boomers retiring, among other challenges that will be faced.

The Capital plays a critical role, where companies are experimenting with new technologies, products and services. Thats why there is a need to allocate new capital to accelerate the innovation and solutions for the new challenges arising around the world.

In LatAm the panorama of funding it's changing with the entrance of titan funds that will uplift competitiveness and might awake interest from other VCs that could potentially invest in the region. (The average ticket invested in México was US$ 1.0 Million while the new fund makes tickets of +US$ 20.0 Million)1
