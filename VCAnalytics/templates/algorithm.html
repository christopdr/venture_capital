Data Decade

Each 10 years, there is a so called "Computing Cycle" that increaces the computing power access by c.10 times.

This started in the 1960s with the main-frame computers which helped companies to scale and increase their global presence, in the 1970s the mini computer came out, in the 1980s the desktop computer helped companies to achieve better efficiency from workers and empowered them.

The 1990s one of the most disruptive cycles came with the creation of the internet, in the 2000 the mobile internet pushed the internet directly to consumers hand, as we go through this current era, the data decade, and companies starting to see value and use their internal data.1

Sources: Equity Research (Data), News Run and MS.
